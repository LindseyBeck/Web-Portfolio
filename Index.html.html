About Me

As a driven student and young woman, I am passionate about honest, creative expression and unique content creation that can tell important stories across several platforms, including social media, visual media, auditory media, and writing. I am currently student at the University of Pittsburgh pursuing a Media and Professional Communication degree with a specialization in Digital Media. I also pursue photography and film production as an additional academic interest and extracurricular hobby.

The power to connect with others and to create engaging, storytelling content into the digital world is now more significant than ever and I want to help tell the story. I have proficient skills in Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft, and Apple programs, as well as an enthusiastic work ethic, attention to detail, ability to multitask, and advanced communication and social skills.

Currently, I am looking for digital media, content creation/management, and film opportunities that will broaden my horizons and challenge me.
